2024 Soaring with Equity, An Aero MLK Event

Soaring with Equity: An Aero MLK Event was hosted in mid-January of 2024 alongside Women in Aeronautics and Astronautics (WAA), Gender Minorities in Aerospace (GeMA), Latinos/Hispanics United in Aerospace (LUNA), and The Office of Multi-Ethnic Student Affairs (MESA). The event featured two main components:
A panel comprised of Michigan Aerospace community members highlighting their experiences in taking steps to consider equity and inclusivity in their day to day work An attendee workshop led by Aerospace Assistant Professor Aaron Johnson that empowered attendees to reflect on ways they could promote inclusivity and equity in their respective spaces
The now annual event was a huge success that offered an opportunity for our Michigan Aerospace community to engage in thoughtful discussion that continues to drive our community forward.

Outreach Event at Chandler Park Academy Middle School

In April of the 2023-2024 academic year, 12 of our members embarked on a full-day of outreach at Chandler Park Academy Middle School in metro Detroit. BSA members presented a 30 minute mini-lesson on planetary orbits, the force of gravity, the properties of light, and the 8 moon phases to several 7th and 8th grade classes, totaling over 180 students. The lesson concluded with a hands-on activity enabling students to build their own solar eclipse viewers ahead of the total solar eclipse that occurred in the days following. BSA members also fielded questions about the college applications process and the possibilities within the world of engineering.
