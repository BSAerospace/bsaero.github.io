Our Membership Development

Member Highlight: Elijah Simpson

Currently, a Junior at the University of Michigan studying Aerospace Engineering and served as BSA president for the 2023-2024 academic year.

Will be returning to work as an Integrated Supply Chain Intern at Blue Origin in Cape Canaveral, FL over Summer 2024.

Selected as a Class of 2022 Patti Grace Smith Fellow and works to help run the fellowship annual summit, empowering Black Excellence in Aerospace.

Led the planning effort and served as moderator for the 2024 Soaring with Equity Aero MLK Event.

Awarded the 2024 North Campus Deans’ MLK Spirit Award for Activism, Awareness & Organization for his work within BSA and Aerospace community at large.

Member Highlight: Miles McGruder

Obtained B.S.E. (2019) in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Michigan.

Presented on two separate technical papers at AIAA’s 2024.

SciTech Conference in Orlando, FL; topics ranged from GPU- accelerated interactive ray casting to turbulent flow reconstruction.

Scheduled to defend his dissertation Summer 2024 in pursuit of a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering with a specialization on Incremental Super-Resolution Reconstruction for Turbulent Flow on High-Order Discontinuous Finite Elements at the University of Michigan.

Plans to enter the Aerospace industry upon graduation as a specialist in computational fluid dynamics.

Member Highlight: Faith Fuller

Currently, a Freshman at the University of Michigan planning to study Aerospace Engineering.

Attended the 2024 NSBE Annual Convention through BSA Funding and secured an internship offer with Northrop Grumman in the company’s Mission Systems sector.

Currently works as an undergraduate research assistant in Professor Anouck Girard's Vehicle Optimization, Dynamics, Control and Autonomy Laboratory.
